<p><p>The Light Crane System market comprises two primary types: Monorail and Double-rail systems. Monorail Light Crane Systems consist of a single track and are designed for lighter loads, offering flexibility and space efficiency in smaller workspaces. In contrast, Double-rail Light Crane Systems feature two parallel tracks, allowing for heavier payloads and increased stability, making them ideal for medium to large-scale operations. Both systems enhance productivity by improving material handling and optimizing workflow in industrial environments.</p></p>
