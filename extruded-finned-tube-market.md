<p><p>The extruded finned tube market primarily consists of two types: single metal tubes and bimetallic tubes. Single metal tubes are made from a single type of metal, providing consistent thermal conductivity and resistance to corrosion. In contrast, bimetallic tubes combine two different metals, enhancing performance by optimizing properties such as strength and thermal efficiency. This dual-material approach allows for improved heat transfer and durability, making bimetallic tubes increasingly popular in applications requiring specific thermal and mechanical characteristics.</p></p>
