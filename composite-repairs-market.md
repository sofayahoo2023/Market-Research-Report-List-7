<p><p>The composite repairs market can be categorized into three main types: structural, semi-structural, and cosmetic. Structural repairs involve restoring the integrity of a load-bearing component, ensuring safety and functionality. Semi-structural repairs address components that bear some load but do not fully support the structure, often enhancing durability. Cosmetic repairs focus on aesthetic improvements, covering flaws without impacting function. Each type serves unique needs, catering to various applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and construction, emphasizing strength, safety, and appearance.</p></p>
