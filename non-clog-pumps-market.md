<p><p>The non-clog pumps market primarily features two types: vortex type and impeller type. Vortex type pumps utilize a swirling motion to lift solids, effectively minimizing clogging, making them suitable for wastewater applications. Impeller type pumps rely on a rotating impeller for fluid movement, designed to handle solids without blocking, ideal for various industrial operations. Both types are essential for efficiently managing challenging fluids while ensuring continuous performance across sectors such as municipal, agricultural, and industrial wastewater management.</p></p>
