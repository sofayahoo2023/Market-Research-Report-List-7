<p><p>The truck-mounted drilling rig market comprises three main types: hydraulic pressure, pneumatic, and power-driven rigs. Hydraulic pressure rigs utilize high-pressure hydraulic systems to power drilling operations, ensuring efficiency and control. Pneumatic rigs use compressed air to drive drilling tools, making them suitable for specific geological conditions. Power-driven rigs rely on traditional mechanical power sources, providing versatility across various applications. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to different drilling requirements and environments, impacting choice in the market.</p></p>
