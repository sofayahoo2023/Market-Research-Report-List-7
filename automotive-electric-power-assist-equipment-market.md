<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Assist Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column EPS</li><li>Pinion EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Electric Power Assist Equipment market primarily comprises two types: Column EPS and Pinion EPS. Column EPS uses an electric motor mounted on the steering column, enhancing steering response and reducing effort for the driver. It typically offers more compact design benefits. In contrast, Pinion EPS is integrated at the pinion gear of the steering rack, delivering superior torque assistance and precise control. Both systems improve vehicle handling while contributing to enhanced fuel efficiency and reduced emissions through lighter overall designs.</p></p>
