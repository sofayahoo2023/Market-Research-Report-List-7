<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li><li>Multi-Stage/ Tandem</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welded hydraulic cylinder market comprises various types based on functionality and design. Single-acting cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure for one-directional movement, relying on external forces for return. Double-acting cylinders allow movement in both directions by utilizing hydraulic pressure on both ends. Multi-stage or tandem cylinders feature multiple nested cylinders enabling higher force and compact design, ideal for applications with space constraints. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing market dynamics and customer preferences across various sectors.</p></p>
