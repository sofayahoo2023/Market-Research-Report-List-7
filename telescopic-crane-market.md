<p><p>A telescopic crane is a type of crane characterized by a boom that consists of multiple tubes fitted within each other, allowing it to extend and retract. The ability to maneuver in tight spaces and its versatility in handling heavy loads contribute to its widespread use across multiple industries.</p><p>The Telescopic Crane Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
