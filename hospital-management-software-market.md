<p><p>The hospital management software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. </p><p>Cloud hospital management software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection. It typically features subscription-based pricing, reducing upfront costs. </p><p>On-premises hospital management software, however, is installed locally on the hospital's infrastructure, providing greater control over data and customization. While it may require a higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance, it can enhance security and compliance for sensitive patient information. </p></p>
