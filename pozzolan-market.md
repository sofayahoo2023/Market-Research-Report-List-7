<p><p>The pozzolan market is divided into two main types: artificial pozzolan and natural pozzolan. Artificial pozzolan is created through industrial processes, such as the calcination of materials like clay or slag, providing specific properties and consistency for construction applications. In contrast, natural pozzolan is sourced from natural deposits, such as volcanic ash or pumice, retaining unique characteristics that enhance concrete performance. Both types are essential for improving durability, lowering environmental impact, and increasing the efficiency of cement-based materials.</p></p>
