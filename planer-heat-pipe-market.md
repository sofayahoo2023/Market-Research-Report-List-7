<p><p>The planar heat pipe market encompasses two main types: standard heat pipes and high conductivity plates. Standard heat pipes are designed for efficient thermal management, utilizing a sealed structure to transfer heat through phase change. They are commonly employed in various industries for cooling electronic components. On the other hand, high conductivity plates enhance heat dissipation by integrating multiple heat pipes within a flat structure, offering superior thermal performance for high-power applications. Both types play crucial roles in advancing thermal solutions.</p></p>
