<p><p>The passenger vehicle propeller shaft market features two main types: rigid shafts and hollow shafts. Rigid shafts are solid components that provide robust support for torque transfer between the transmission and the drive axle, ensuring durability and reliability. In contrast, hollow shafts are lighter and often used for their reduced weight, which enhances vehicle efficiency and performance. Both types play a critical role in optimizing the power transfer in passenger vehicles, with specific applications depending on the vehicle design and performance requirements.</p></p>
