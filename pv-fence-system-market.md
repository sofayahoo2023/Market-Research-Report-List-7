<p><p>The PV fence system market can be categorized into two main types: column type and panel type. Column type PV fences consist of vertical posts that support solar panels, providing flexibility in design and installation while optimizing land use. In contrast, panel type PV fences integrate solar panels directly into pre-fabricated fencing structures, offering efficient use of space and enhanced aesthetics. Both types serve to harness solar energy while providing security and enclosure, catering to different installation needs and site conditions.</p></p>
