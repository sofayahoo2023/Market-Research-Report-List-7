<p><p>Ship davits are essential boat handling devices used in maritime operations, and they come in three main types. Hydraulic davits utilize hydraulic systems for lifting and lowering boats, offering powerful and efficient operation. Electric davits rely on electric motors, providing ease of use and lower maintenance requirements. Manual davits require physical manpower for operation, often offering simplicity and reliability in smaller vessels. Each type serves specific needs based on vessel size, weight capacity, and operational requirements.</p></p>
