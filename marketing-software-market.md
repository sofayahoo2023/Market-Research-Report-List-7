<p><p>Marketing software is categorized into cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based marketing software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, remote access, and reduced IT maintenance. This model is often subscription-based, allowing businesses to pay as they grow. On-premise marketing software, in contrast, is installed locally on a company's hardware, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring a higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types serve different business needs and resources.</p></p>
