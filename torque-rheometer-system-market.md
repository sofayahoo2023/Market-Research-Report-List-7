<p><p>The Torque Rheometer System Market is primarily segmented into two types: Single Screw Rheometers and Twin Screw Rheometers. Single Screw Rheometers utilize a single screw for processing and measuring the material's viscosity and flow characteristics, making them suitable for specific applications with lower throughput. In contrast, Twin Screw Rheometers feature two intermeshing screws, providing better mixing, higher shear rates, and enhanced material processing capabilities, which cater to a wider range of applications and increased operational efficiency in polymer processing and compounding.</p></p>
