<p><p>The rigid-flex PCB market comprises three main types: single layer, double layer, and multi-layer. Single-layer PCBs offer a simplified design with one conductive layer, ideal for basic applications. Double-layer PCBs incorporate two conductive layers, enhancing connectivity and complexity for more advanced devices. Multi-layer PCBs feature three or more layers, allowing for intricate circuits and higher functionality in compact designs, making them suitable for high-performance electronics. Each type caters to specific industry needs based on complexity, size, and performance requirements.</p></p>
