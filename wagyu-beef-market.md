<p><p>Wagyu beef is categorized into three main market types: Fullblood Wagyu, Purebred Wagyu, and Crossbred Wagyu. Fullblood Wagyu is the highest quality, derived from genetically pure Japanese lines, ensuring superior marbling and flavor. Purebred Wagyu consists of a minimum percentage of Wagyu genetics, maintaining substantial quality but with slightly less pedigree. Crossbred Wagyu involves mixing Wagyu with other breeds, offering a more affordable option with improved muscle growth, yet still retaining some of the desirable characteristics of Wagyu beef.</p></p>
