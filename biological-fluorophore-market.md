<p><p>The biological fluorophore market consists of various types, including Green Fluorescent Protein (GFP), Phycoerythrin, and Allophycocyanin. GFP is widely used in live-cell imaging due to its strong fluorescence and stability. Phycoerythrin, derived from red algae, exhibits high brightness and is commonly used in flow cytometry. Allophycocyanin is another algal-derived fluorophore known for its excellent light absorption and stability, making it suitable for multiplex immunoassays. These fluorophores are essential in biomedical research, diagnostics, and imaging applications.</p></p>
