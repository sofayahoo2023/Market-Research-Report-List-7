<p><p>The centrifugal sieves market is divided into two main types: single centrifugal chamber and multiple centrifugal chamber systems. Single centrifugal chamber sieves feature one chamber that utilizes centrifugal force to separate particles based on size, making them effective for simpler applications. In contrast, multiple centrifugal chamber systems have several chambers that allow for enhanced separation efficiency and versatility, catering to more complex processes and higher throughput requirements. Both types are crucial in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals for material classification.</p></p>
