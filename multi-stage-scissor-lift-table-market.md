<p><p>The multi-stage scissor lift table market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic lift tables use compressed air for operation, offering quick lift cycles and minimal maintenance, making them suitable for environments requiring clean operations. Hydraulic lift tables use fluid pressure to lift heavy loads, providing greater lifting capacity and stability, ideal for industrial settings. Electric lift tables utilize electric motors for powered lifting, offering precision control and efficiency, often favored in warehouses and retail applications.</p></p>
