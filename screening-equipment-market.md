<p><p>The screening equipment market is primarily divided into two types: fixed screeners and mobile screeners. Fixed screeners are installed at a specific location for long-term material separation, often used in large-scale operations like mines and quarries. Mobile screeners, on the other hand, are designed for flexibility and mobility, allowing for quick repositioning on job sites. They are ideal for smaller projects or locations requiring frequent setup changes. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, facilitating efficient material handling.</p></p>
