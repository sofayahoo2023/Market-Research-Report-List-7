<p><strong>The Medical & Hygiene Non Woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical and hygiene non-woven fabric market comprises four main types: drylaid, airlaid, wetlaid, and spunlaid. </p><p>Drylaid involves fibers that are carded and bonded, suitable for products like surgical gowns. Airlaid uses air to disperse fibers, creating absorbent materials for hygiene items. Wetlaid employs water to mix and form sheets of fibers, commonly used in wipes. Spunlaid combines molten fibers formed into webs, offering strength and versatility for medical applications, including protective clothing and disposable drapes.</p></p>
