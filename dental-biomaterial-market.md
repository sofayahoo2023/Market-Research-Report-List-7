<p><p>The dental biomaterial market encompasses two primary types: natural dental products and biocompatible synthetic dental products. Natural dental products, derived from natural sources like collagen or chitosan, offer inherent biocompatibility and promote healing. In contrast, biocompatible synthetic dental products are engineered materials designed to mimic natural tissues while providing durability and stability. These synthetic options often include polymers and ceramics, suitable for various dental applications such as fillings, implants, and prosthetics, catering to the evolving needs of dental professionals and patients.</p></p>
