<p><ul><li>SHAB</li><li>SHB</li><li>LSA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The square flanges market is categorized into three main types: SHAB (Standard High-Pressure Adjustable Bolting), SHB (Standard High-Pressure Bolting), and LSA (Lightweight Slip-On Flange). SHAB flanges are designed for high-pressure applications with adjustable features for enhanced sealing. SHB flanges cater to high-pressure needs but without adjustability, making them suitable for fixed applications. LSA flanges are lightweight and ideal for low-pressure uses, facilitating ease of installation in various industrial settings, thereby accommodating a wide range of operational requirements.</p></p>
