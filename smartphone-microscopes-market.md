<p><p>The smartphone microscope market is primarily categorized into two types: single tube microscopes and multi tube microscopes. Single tube microscopes feature a single optical path for viewing specimens, offering simplicity and portability, making them ideal for casual users and educational purposes. Multi tube microscopes, on the other hand, have multiple optical paths, allowing for complex viewing options and enhanced image quality. They cater to more advanced applications, such as research and professional use, where detailed analysis is crucial.</p></p>
