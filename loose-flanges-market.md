<p><strong>The Loose Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF</li><li>FM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The loose flanges market consists primarily of two types: RF (Raised Face) and FM (Flat Machined). RF flanges feature a raised surface that enhances sealing efficiency in high-pressure applications, commonly used in piping systems. In contrast, FM flanges offer a flat sealing surface, suited for lower pressure requirements and providing a simpler connection. Both types cater to various sectors, including oil and gas, water treatment, and construction, reflecting the diverse needs for reliable and efficient joint connections in industrial applications.</p></p>
