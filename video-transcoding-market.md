<p><strong>The Video Transcoding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>H264</li><li>H265</li><li>VP9</li><li>AV1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video transcoding market is dominated by several key codecs: H.264, H.265, VP9, and AV1. H.264 remains widely used for its balance between compression and quality, ideal for streaming. H.265 offers improved compression efficiency for 4K video but requires more processing power. VP9, developed by Google, optimizes video for web delivery with open-source benefits. AV1 is the newest, focusing on delivering high-quality video at lower bitrates, gaining traction in streaming services due to its royalty-free licensing.</p></p>
