<p><p>The Industrial Vacuum Distillation Units market is categorized into two main types: dry type and wet type. Dry type units primarily handle non-aqueous solutions, enabling efficient separation of volatiles without relying on water. They are suited for high-purity applications. In contrast, wet type units use water or steam during the distillation process, making them ideal for processing liquid mixtures containing water-soluble components. Both types are crucial in various industries for solvent recovery, petrochemical processing, and environmental applications.</p></p>
