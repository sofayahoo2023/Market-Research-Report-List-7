<p><p>The Portable Alcohol Detector market encompasses various sensor types, primarily including Fuel Cell Alcohol Sensors, Semiconductor Alcohol Sensors, and Others. Fuel Cell Alcohol Sensors offer high accuracy and reliability by oxidizing alcohol vapor to generate an electrical current, ideal for law enforcement and safety applications. Semiconductor Alcohol Sensors utilize conductive materials that change resistance in response to alcohol exposure, making them cost-effective yet less accurate. The "Others" category includes alternative technologies like infrared sensors, catering to niche applications and specialized markets.</p></p>
