<p><p>The fiber-optic hydrophone market comprises primarily two types: dual-mode fiber and multimode fiber. Dual-mode fiber hydrophones utilize both acoustic and electromagnetic signals, enhancing sensitivity and performance in various environments, especially in underwater applications. Multimode fiber hydrophones, on the other hand, support multiple light paths, which allows for increased signal transmission capacity but can compromise accuracy in certain applications. Together, these technologies cater to diverse needs in naval, environmental monitoring, and industrial sectors, optimizing performance and reliability.</p></p>
