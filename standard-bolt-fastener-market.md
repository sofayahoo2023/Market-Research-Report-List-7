<p><p>The standard bolt market comprises various types of fasteners, including half screw bolts and full screw bolts. Half screw bolts feature a partially threaded shaft, providing a combination of grip and ease of assembly, ideal for applications requiring quick fastening. In contrast, full screw bolts are fully threaded, offering maximum tension and load distribution, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types serve essential roles in construction and manufacturing, catering to diverse industry needs while ensuring durability and reliability in fastening solutions.</p></p>
