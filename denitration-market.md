<p><p>The denitration market is segmented into three main types: cellular, plate, and corrugated. Cellular type denitrators utilize a honeycomb structure to maximize contact between gases and the catalyst, enhancing efficiency. Plate type denitrators leverage stacked plates to create turbulence, improving mass transfer and reaction rates. Corrugated type denitrators employ a wavy structure to increase surface area, facilitating better gas flow and contact with the catalyst. Each type offers unique advantages in optimizing nitrogen removal in various applications.</p></p>
