<p><p>The semiconductor testing equipment market is divided into two main types: testing equipment before packaging and testing equipment after packaging. Testing before packaging focuses on evaluating semiconductor chips during fabrication to ensure they meet specifications and performance standards. This involves assessing functionality and reliability before encapsulation. In contrast, testing after packaging involves evaluating the finished semiconductor devices to confirm their operational integrity and reliability in real-world applications, ensuring they function correctly in electronic systems. Both types are essential for quality assurance in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
