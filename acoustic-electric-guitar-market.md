<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Pickup</li><li>Piezo Pickup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic-electric guitar market features two main types of pickups: electromagnetic and piezo. Electromagnetic pickups utilize magnets to capture string vibrations, translating them into electrical signals, commonly found in archtop and hollow-body guitars. In contrast, piezo pickups sense vibrations directly from the guitar's body or bridge, offering a more natural sound and responsiveness, making them ideal for acoustic styles. Both pickup types cater to different player preferences and playing environments, influencing sound quality and amplification in performances.</p></p>
