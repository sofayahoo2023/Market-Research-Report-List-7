<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multiple Point</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace engine fuel nozzle market is divided into two main types: single-point and multiple-point fuel nozzles. Single-point nozzles deliver fuel from a single location, simplifying design and enhancing reliability, often used in smaller engines. In contrast, multiple-point nozzles feature several outlets, ensuring more uniform fuel distribution, improving combustion efficiency, and enabling better performance in larger, more complex engines. Each type serves specific operational requirements based on the engine size, design complexity, and desired performance characteristics.</p></p>
