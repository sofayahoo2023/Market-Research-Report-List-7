Three-stage rocker provides explosive pop off the wake and is favored by advanced riders for tricks. Five-stage rocker includes more pronounced separation between rocker sections, enabling aggressive maneuvers and smoother landings. Each type caters to different skill levels and riding styles.</p></p>
