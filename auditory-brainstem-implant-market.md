<p><p>The Auditory Brainstem Implant (ABI) market comprises three essential components: microphones, decoding chips, and electrodes. Microphones capture sound, translating it into electrical signals for processing. Decoding chips analyze these signals, converting them into a format suitable for stimulation. Finally, electrodes deliver these processed signals directly to the brainstem, bypassing damaged auditory pathways. Together, these elements enhance auditory perception for individuals with severe hearing loss or those who cannot benefit from traditional cochlear implants, driving innovation and growth in the market.</p></p>
