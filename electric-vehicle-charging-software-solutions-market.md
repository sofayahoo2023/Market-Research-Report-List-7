<p><p>Electric Vehicle Charging Software Solutions are categorized into three main types. Charging-point software manages the operation of charging stations, facilitating user access, payment processing, and real-time monitoring of charging sessions. Back-end/infrastructure software oversees the entire network, coordinating data flow, managing resources, and providing analytics to enhance the efficiency and reliability of charging infrastructure on a broader scale.</p></p>
