<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-etching Adhesive</li><li>Selective-etching Adhesive</li><li>Total-etching Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental adhesives market comprises three main types: self-etching, selective-etching, and total-etching adhesives. Self-etching adhesives simplify the bonding process by simultaneously etching and priming the tooth surface, reducing technique sensitivity. Selective-etching adhesives involve etching only the enamel, allowing for a stronger bond while preserving dentin integrity. Total-etching adhesives require etching of both enamel and dentin, offering a strong bond but demanding more meticulous application. Each type caters to specific clinical needs and preferences, impacting their market demand.</p></p>
