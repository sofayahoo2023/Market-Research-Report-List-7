<p><p>Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS) Analyzers are sophisticated analytical tools that utilize laser technology to determine the elemental composition of various materials. This technique involves focusing a high-energy laser pulse onto a sample, creating a plasma plume that emits light characteristic of the elements present. LIBS is favored for its rapid analysis, minimal sample preparation requirements, and ability to work with solids, liquids, and gases. </p><p>The market for LIBS Analyzers is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for real-time and on-site analysis across various industries such as metals, mining, environmental monitoring, and pharmaceuticals.
